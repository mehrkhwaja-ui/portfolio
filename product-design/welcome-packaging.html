Product Design — UBL

New Customer Welcome Packaging: a bank that gets you, not your grandfather's bank.

UBL is one of Pakistan's oldest banks, and it looked like it — conservative, formal, built for businessmen in suits. I redesigned the welcome package new customers receive to signal that this bank is for everyone.

Role
Product Designer
Company
UBL
Timeline
February 2024
Skills
Packaging Design, Print Design, Brand Positioning

01 Goal

Shift perception from "your grandfather's bank" to "a bank that gets you."

The welcome package new customers received reinforced the old perception: beige, bureaucratic, boring. It was often the first physical object a new customer held from the bank — and it was saying the opposite of what UBL wanted to be.

02 Solution

I redesigned the packaging to be colorful, approachable, and human — a small intervention, but one that changed the first impression thousands of new customers received.

The redesigned welcome package — colorful and approachable.

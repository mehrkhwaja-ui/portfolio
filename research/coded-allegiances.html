Research & Critical Design — with Samvit

Coded Allegiances

We created a tarot-style card system that transforms abstract actors in surveillance capitalism — algorithms, data brokers, ad networks — into identifiable characters with motivations, relationships, and power dynamics. Think: personifying the invisible infrastructure that tracks you.

Speculative Design Critical Design Research Systems Design Card Design

Surveillance capitalism is deliberately opaque. Platforms, data brokers, and algorithms hide behind jargon and legalese, making it nearly impossible to understand how our data is collected, sold, and weaponized.

The Method

We queered the "controller." Instead of accepting surveillance capitalism's language on its own terms, we built our own metaphorical system — one grounded in storytelling, emotion, and play rather than technical obfuscation.

Why It Matters

Making something legible is a political act. It reclaims agency, invites participation, and creates space for people excluded by dominant narratives. This project was both critique and tool: a way to help people not just understand the systems they're part of, but to question and resist them.

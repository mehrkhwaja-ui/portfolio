Red Flags: A T&C Quiz

In February 2025, I got really into Meta's terms and conditions, so I designed a quiz. We spend hours daily with social media platforms: they know our secrets, influence our moods, track our movements. They're relationships. So I reframed terms and conditions as relationship red flags.

Metaphors Print Design Privacy Policy Research User Awareness

"Can I share your private messages with third parties?" becomes "Can I read your diary and tell my friends about it?" The quiz makes the absurdity visceral. Suddenly, what we casually agree to when we click "Accept" feels as uncomfortable as it should.
